About Us

a soccer quiz website allowing people to win R5000 if they successfully answer 18 questions (correctly). The questions being answered are based on the outcome of a single soccer match (Chelsea vs Liverpool).

Questions are in a multiple choice format and each question will have 10 possible answers.

The R5000 being available to be won is pool money; meaning if there are multiple winners, they will share it equally.

Playing will be free but those playing for free will be unable to pick some of the answers (options). Those playing for free will not be able to pick the first three answers (A-0, B-1, C-2) from each question. Example below
